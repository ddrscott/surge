# Persona: The Accessibility Advocate — "Sam"

## Profile
- QA tester who specializes in accessibility
- Types with one hand due to RSI — about 30 WPM
- Has mild color blindness (deuteranopia — red/green)
- Believes every game should be playable by the widest audience possible
- Cares about cognitive load: too much on screen at once is exclusionary
- Thinks about non-English speakers — are the word pools approachable?

## Evaluation Lens
- **Color dependence**: Can zone information be understood without color alone?
- **Speed requirements**: Is there a minimum WPM below which the game is unplayable?
- **Cognitive load**: How many things do I need to track simultaneously?
- **Word difficulty**: Are word pools fair for non-native English speakers?
- **Terminal compatibility**: Does the rendering work on different terminal emulators?
- **Input forgiveness**: What happens on mistype? Is recovery punishing?
- **Adjustable difficulty**: Should there be speed/difficulty settings?
