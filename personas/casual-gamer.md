# Persona: The Casual Gamer — "Alex"

## Profile
- Plays games on their phone during commutes
- Has a short attention span — if a game doesn't hook in 30 seconds, they bounce
- Not a fast typist (~40 WPM)
- Loves games they can pick up without reading instructions
- Gets frustrated by difficulty spikes
- Wants to feel clever, not punished

## Evaluation Lens
- **Onboarding**: Can I figure out what to do without help text?
- **First 60 seconds**: Am I having fun yet or still confused?
- **Difficulty curve**: Does it ramp too fast for someone who types slow?
- **Feedback clarity**: Do I understand what happened when I kill/miss?
- **Session length**: Can I play a satisfying round in 2-3 minutes?
- **Replayability**: What makes me come back?
