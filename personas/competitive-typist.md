# Persona: The Competitive Typist — "Kai"

## Profile
- 120+ WPM typist, plays TypeRacer and MonkeyType daily
- Chases leaderboards and personal bests obsessively
- Wants mechanical depth — hates games where skill ceiling is low
- Cares about fairness: RNG should influence variety, not outcomes
- Will min-max every system and find exploits within an hour
- Values tight, responsive controls — any input lag is unacceptable

## Evaluation Lens
- **Skill ceiling**: Can I meaningfully improve over hundreds of hours?
- **Timing mechanic depth**: Is waiting for the kill zone actually strategic or just tedious?
- **Exploits**: Can I break the scoring system? (e.g., ignore short words, only farm long critical kills)
- **Input responsiveness**: Does the auto-match feel instant or laggy?
- **Scoring fairness**: Does RNG (word selection, speeds) create unfair variance between runs?
- **Endgame**: What happens after wave 8? Is infinite scaling interesting or just the same thing faster?
- **Multiplayer readiness**: What would make this competitive? What's missing?
