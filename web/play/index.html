How to Play

Risk = Reward More danger, more points. Hackers like danger. danger 3x ··· close 2x ··· safe 1x.
Combos Chain kills without missing. Every 5 kills boosts your multiplier. One miss resets it.
Power-ups patch fix = heal   freeze = slow bugs   optimize = 2x score. They move fast.
Surge Meter Kills fill the surge bar. When full, type surge to wipe the screen.
Typing Start typing any visible bug name to lock on. Backspace to correct. Esc to cancel.
Waves Each wave brings faster, more numerous bugs. Rest between waves to catch your breath.
